What we do

The Detroit Bridal Shower Project has helped many newly married couples start off their homes and begin their new life together with ease. Our website creates a platform where the community can view upcoming engagements and the hostesses of each simcha. Through our website you can add a newly engaged couple to the mailing list as well as have the option of having a personal shopper take care of the shopping on your behalf. We also provide a shopping guide for those who are interested in shopping on their own. Thank you to the Detroit community for making all of this possible!
