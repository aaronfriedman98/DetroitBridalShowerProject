What we do

The Detroit Bridal Shower Project has helped tons of newly married couples start off their homes and begin their new life together with ease. Our website makes the process of notifying the community of upcoming engagements and viewing the hostesses of each simcha so much easier. Through our website you can add a new engaged couple to the mailing list and have the option of having a personal shopper take care of the shopping on your behalf. The website also provides a shopping guide to help those who are interested in shopping on their own. Thank you to the Detroit community for making this all possible!
